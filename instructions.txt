Version 1.1.1

INSTALLATION
1. launch minecraft 1.14
2. create a world you want minecarts with flame decals to be in then save/quit
3. click "edit" after selecting the world
4. click "open world folder"
5. open the datapacks folder
4. drag the zip file into here then launch the world!

HOW TO USE
Place your rails on soul soil, build up a bit of speed, then enjoy minecarts at 2.5x speed!
